About Our Company

Trusted Maritime Partners

Incorporated in 2008 • Based in Goa, India

Meti Shipping Management Private Limited has been active in transport, shipping logistics, and storage since 2008. From our primary hub in Goa, we administer a comprehensive network of maritime freight, custom warehousing solutions, and international shipping operations.

We coordinate dry and temperature-controlled cargo, customs clearances, supply chain optimization, and vessel chartering to streamline your shipping needs, keeping operations safety-focused and efficient.
