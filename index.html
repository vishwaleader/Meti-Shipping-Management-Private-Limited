About Us

A Legacy Forged on International Waters

Based in Goa, India • Serving Global Routes

Founded in 2008, Meti Shipping Management Private Limited has grown into a highly recognized maritime transport and storage services company. We combine deep operational expertise with an unwavering commitment to safety, compliance, and client success.

Today, our teams manage cargo flows, port clearances, and complex supply chain logistics across major global ports — supporting bulk transport, container shipments, and warehousing requirements with professional precision.

  • ISO 9001 & ISM Certified Operations
  • Dedicated 24/7 Operations Desk
  • Secure Bonded Warehouses
  • Customs Clearance Integration
